Design System

Lexicon is a set of principles, patterns and tools created to provide a common design framework for crafting user interfaces within Liferay product ecosystem.

+

Modular Approach

The lexicon approach to interface design is based on modularity. A flexible and consistent system that is able to respond to the multiple needs of modern digital products.

+

Open Platform

An open space for designers, developers or any other person involved in the creation of digital products who want to contribute with their own proposals and suggestions.
